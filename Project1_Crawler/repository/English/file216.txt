&lt;td&gt;&lt;div lang="en" dir="ltr" class="description en" style="display:inline;"&gt;&lt;i&gt;Coney Island at Night&lt;/i&gt;, a short silent film from 1905, by director and cameraman &lt;a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Edwin_S._Porter" class="extiw" title="w:Edwin S.
