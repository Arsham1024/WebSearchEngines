               <p>
                <b>
                 Rhine
                </b>
                , 39th jahaaj rahaa jisme girmitya logan ke India se Fiji lawa gais rahaa. Ii jahaaj 1,691 ton garrhu, loha ke banaa paal waala jahaaj rahaa jiske Port Glasgow ke Russel &amp; Company, Nourse Line khatir banais rahaa aur jiske 10 December 1885 ke launch karaa gais rahaa. Ii jahaaj 257.2 lamba, 38.3 feet chaurra aur 23.1 feet gahiraa rahaa aur iske naam Germany ke Rhine nadii se aais hai. Iske jaada kar ke girmitya logan ke British colonies me lae jae khatir kaam me lawa jaat rahaa.
                <a href="/wiki/30_August" title="30 August">
                 30 August
                </a>
                <a href="/wiki/1900" title="1900">
                 1900
                </a>
                ke Rhine 491 girmitya logan ke lae ke Fiji aais rahaa. 1907 me Rhine ke New Brunswick, Canada ke R.C. Williams ke becha gais rahaa. 1909 me iske fir se W.H. Chandler ke becha gais, jon, Rhine Shipping Company, Montreal, Canada me chalaat rahaa. 1911 me Rhine Shipping Company ke aur 1915 me Boston ke W. McKissock ke aur 1923 me E.P. Reiss ke becha gais.

                  <b>
                   <a href="/wiki/Fiji_Crested_Iguana" title="Fiji Crested Iguana">
                    Fiji Crested Iguana
                   </a>
                  </b>
                  (
                  <i>
                   Brachylophus vitiensis
                  </i>
                  ), Fiji me dui rakam ke iguana me se ek hai. Duusra iguana hai,
                  <a class="extiw" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Banded_iguana" title="w:Banded iguana">
                   Banded iguana
                  </a>
                  (
                  <i>
                   Brachylophus fasciatus
                  </i>
                  ). Duuno species iguana ke ek species me se evolve bhain hai jon ki South America se raft me bahe ke Fiji pahucha rahin. Fiji Crested Iguana ke
                  <a href="/wiki/1979" title="1979">
                   1979
                  </a>
                  me pahila dafe, University of the South Pacific ke Dr John Gibbons paais rahaa. Fiji Crested Iguana ke Banded iguana se antar iske male pe tiin patra ujjar band se karaa jaae sake hai jab ki Banded iguana me mota, chakla, blue band rahe hai. Fiji Crested Iguana duusra species se baraa hai, iske pithi me spiny "crest" hai aur ii aapan rang badal ke karia kar le sake hai. Ii iguana ke khali
                  <a href="/wiki/Yadua_Taba" title="Yadua Taba">
                   Yadua Taba
                  </a>
                  Island me pawa jaae hai kahe ki huan pe bakrri nai hai jon ki iguana ke rahe waala jagah ke barbaad kar de hai.
