      <p>
       <br/>
       <b>
        Järva-Jaani
       </b>
       is a
       <a href="/wiki/Populated_places_in_Estonia" title="Populated places in Estonia">
        borough
       </a>
       (
       <a href="/wiki/Estonian_language" title="Estonian language">
        Estonian
       </a>
       :
       <i lang="et">
        alev
       </i>
       ) in
       <a href="/wiki/J%C3%A4rva_County" title="Järva County">
        Järva County
       </a>
       , in
       <a href="/wiki/J%C3%A4rva_Parish" title="Järva Parish">
        Järva Parish
       </a>
       , central
       <a href="/wiki/Estonia" title="Estonia">
        Estonia
       </a>
       .
       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-stat.ee_1-0">
        <a href="#cite_note-stat.ee-1">
         [1]
        </a>
       </sup>
       It was the administrative center of
       <a href="/wiki/J%C3%A4rva-Jaani_Parish" title="Järva-Jaani Parish">
        Järva-Jaani Parish
       </a>
       .
      </p>
