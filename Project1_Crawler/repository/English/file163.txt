       <header class="hero__header">
        <div class="hero__text-column">
         <small class="hero__kicker">
          Advocacy
         </small>
         <h1 class="hero__title">
          We champion everyone’s right to free knowledge online.
         </h1>
        </div>
        <figure class="hero__image-container image-filter-inherit">
         <img alt="" class="hero__image" src="https://wikimediafoundation.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Wikimedia_Foundation_SOPA_War_Room_Meeting_1-17-2012-1-9-e1533254929224.jpg"/>
        </figure>
       </header>
       <div class="hero__intro">
        <p>
         We protect users’ privacy and speech. We educate governments and policymakers about the importance of the free and open internet. Regardless of location or circumstance, everyone has the right to seek and share knowledge.
        </p>
       </div>
      </div>
      <div class="wp-block-shiro-stairs">
       <div class="wp-block-shiro-stair stair">
        <h2 class="stair__heading is-style-h3">
         Protecting the right to speak and learn freely
        </h2>
        <img class="wp-image-61703 size-image_16x9_small stair__image" height="338" loading="lazy" src="https://wikimediafoundation.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/Censored_section_of_Green_Illusions_by_Ozzie_Zehner.jpg?w=600&amp;h=338&amp;crop=1" width="600"/>
        <p class="stair__body">
         <a href="https://policy.wikimedia.org/">
          We shape public policy
         </a>
         that opens access to free online knowledge around the globe. Government imposed censorship and overzealous copyright laws should not stand in the way of international knowledge-sharing.
        </p>
       </div>
       <div class="wp-block-shiro-stair stair">
        <h2 class="stair__heading is-style-h3">
         Defending free expression
        </h2>
        <img class="wp-image-61192 size-image_16x9_small stair__image" height="338" loading="lazy" src="https://wikimediafoundation.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/Transparency_Report_1-e1565971921744.jpg?w=600&amp;h=338&amp;crop=1" width="600"/>
        <p class="stair__body">
         Last year we received 684 takedown requests and granted zero. Governments and individuals often ask us to change project content or share information about our users. We push back when these requests could harm users’ rights. We tell you about these requests in our
         <a href="https://transparency.wikimedia.org/">
          Transparency Report
         </a>
         .
        </p>
       </div>
       <div class="wp-block-shiro-stair stair">
        <h2 class="stair__heading is-style-h3">
         Standing up for our users
        </h2>
        <img class="wp-image-37982 size-image_16x9_small stair__image" height="338" loading="lazy" src="https://wikimediafoundation.org/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/frankfurt_am_main-gerechtigkeitsbrunnen-detail-justitia_von_nordwesten-20110411-cropped-1024px.jpg?w=600&amp;h=338&amp;crop=1" width="600"/>
        <p class="stair__body">
         We help contributors respond to legal threats relating to their work on Wikimedia projects. We have joined legal efforts like the
         <a href="https://wikimediafoundation.org/2017/02/06/amicus-brief-immigration-travel-restrictions/">
          opposition to the 2017 US travel ban
         </a>
         . We are
         <a href="https://policy.wikimedia.org/stopsurveillance/">
          suing the United States National Security Agency
         </a>
         , challenging their mass surveillance of Wikimedia users and contributors.
        </p>
       </div>
       <div class="wp-block-shiro-stair stair">
        <h2 class="stair__heading is-style-h3">
         Supporting community voices
        </h2>
        <img class="wp-image-57346 size-image_16x9_small stair__image" height="338" loading="lazy" src="https://wikimediafoundation.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/07/Felix_Satdeep_Stalinjeet_and_Manavpreet_at_Wikimedia_Conference_2017-e1532738694295.jpg?w=600&amp;h=338&amp;crop=1" width="600"/>
        <p class="stair__body">
         Volunteer contributors take the lead on editorial decisions. Our
         <a href="https://blog.wikimedia.org/2017/11/09/cda-230-principles-wikipedia/">
          public policy efforts
         </a>
         and
         <a href="https://wikimediafoundation.org/2017/07/12/intermediary-liability-protections-research-initiative/">
          research initiatives
         </a>
         focus on maintaining the legal protections that make that possible.
        </p>
       </div>
       <div class="wp-block-shiro-stair stair">
        <h2 class="stair__heading is-style-h3">
         Speak up for free knowledge and access
        </h2>
        <img class="wp-image-61196 size-image_16x9_small stair__image" height="338" loading="lazy" src="https://wikimediafoundation.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/supertodada_video_excerpt-e1565971784965.png?w=600&amp;h=338&amp;crop=1" width="600"/>
        <p class="stair__body">
         Just 27% of internet users in Nigeria had heard of Wikipedia in 2016. Today,
         <a href="https://wikimediafoundation.org/2017/09/21/57141/">
          that number is up to 48%
         </a>
         . Our organization is committed to increasing awareness of Wikipedia outside of North America and Europe, in order to expand global knowledge participation.
        </p>
       </div>
       <div class="wp-block-shiro-stair stair">
        <h2 class="stair__heading is-style-h3">
         Fight legal battles to make free knowledge possible
        </h2>
        <img class="wp-image-61193 size-image_16x9_small stair__image" height="338" loading="lazy" src="https://wikimediafoundation.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/Albert_V_Bryan_Federal_District_Courthouse_-_Alexandria_Va-e1565971808499.jpg?w=600&amp;h=338&amp;crop=1" width="600"/>
        <p class="stair__body">
         Access to knowledge around the world is under constant attack. At Wikimedia, we work to give access to knowledge to everyone, for free, forever. We fight against

         , and fiercely promote open licenses to ensure our materials can be used and revised globally.
