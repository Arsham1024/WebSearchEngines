       <header class="hero__header">
        <div class="hero__text-column">
         <small class="hero__kicker">
          Our Work
         </small>
         <h1 class="hero__title">
          We help everyone share in the sum of all knowledge.
         </h1>
        </div>
        <figure class="hero__image-container image-filter-inherit">
         <img alt="" class="hero__image" src="https://wikimediafoundation.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/KS_edit-a-thon_2017-02-a_jeh-e1533254882601.jpg?w=1024&amp;h=675&amp;crop=1"/>
        </figure>
       </header>
       <div class="hero__intro">
        <p>
         We are the people who keep knowledge free. There is an amazing community of people around the world that makes great projects like Wikipedia. We help them do that work. We take care of the technical infrastructure, the legal challenges, and the growing pains.
        </p>
       </div>
      </div>
      <div class="wp-block-shiro-stairs">
       <div class="wp-block-shiro-stair stair">
        <h2 class="stair__heading is-style-h3">
         Host free knowledge projects
        </h2>
        <img class="wp-image-61198 size-image_16x9_small stair__image" height="338" loading="lazy" src="https://wikimediafoundation.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/Wikimedia_Foundation_Servers_2015-63-e1565971769756.jpg?w=600&amp;h=338&amp;crop=1" width="600"/>
        <p class="stair__body">
         The Wikimedia Foundation was established as a nonprofit in 2003 to give Wikipedia a permanent home. We now
         <a href="/our-work/wikimedia-projects/">
          host 13 collaborative knowledge projects
         </a>
         from
         <a href="/our-work/wikipedia/">
          Wikipedia
         </a>
         , the free encyclopedia, to
         <a href="/our-work/wikidata/">
          Wikidata
         </a>
         , a set of structured data, and beyond. We maintain the servers, build the software, and design the technology that keep these projects running.
        </p>
       </div>
       <div class="wp-block-shiro-stair stair">
        <h2 class="stair__heading is-style-h3">
         Deliver grants for good
        </h2>
        <img class="wp-image-61195 size-image_16x9_small stair__image" height="338" loading="lazy" src="https://wikimediafoundation.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/Skrivstuga_ArtAndFeminism_Stockholm_09-e1565971792652.jpg?w=600&amp;h=338&amp;crop=1" width="600"/>
        <p class="stair__body">
         We partner with Wikimedia communities around the world to provide them with the support they need to do great work. Last year we awarded over 9 million USD in grants to Wikimedia community members, affiliates and nonprofit organizations.
         <a href="https://meta.wikimedia.org/wiki/Grants:Start">
          We fund
         </a>
         editathons, outreach efforts, events, and conferences. We help them get the word out for free knowledge.
        </p>
       </div>
       <div class="wp-block-shiro-stair stair">
        <h2 class="stair__heading is-style-h3">
         Grow community around the world
        </h2>
        <img class="wp-image-61197 size-image_16x9_small stair__image" height="338" loading="lazy" src="https://wikimediafoundation.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/Wikimania_2016_-_group_photo_02-e1565971776858.jpg?w=600&amp;h=338&amp;crop=1" width="600"/>
        <p class="stair__body">
         When more people can access and contribute to free knowledge, the better that knowledge reflects the world. We are committed to helping grow and sustain a healthy community of over 220,000 people across 170 nations. From the
         <a href="https://meta.wikimedia.org/wiki/Community_health_initiative">
          community health initiative
         </a>
         to
         <a href="/2015/11/30/artificial-intelligence-x-ray-specs/">
          ORES
         </a>
         and
         <a href="https://wikimediafoundation.org/our-work/what-wikipedia-is-doing-about-gender-diversity/">
          our work in gender diversity
         </a>
         , the Foundation is actively working to make our projects more inclusive.
        </p>
       </div>
       <div class="wp-block-shiro-stair stair">
        <h2 class="stair__heading is-style-h3">
         Support education and learning worldwide
        </h2>
        <img class="wp-image-63085 size-image_16x9_small stair__image" height="338" loading="lazy" src="https://wikimediafoundation.org/wp-content/uploads/2020/07/Club_de_Edición_II_mayo_2017.jpg?w=600&amp;h=338&amp;crop=1" width="600"/>
        <p class="stair__body">
         We
         <a href="/our-work/education">
          help teachers and students
         </a>
         of all ages access and use the full potential of Wikimedia projects for their learning objectives. Key pillars of our education work include promoting knowledge equity and teaching 21st century skills.
        </p>
       </div>
       <div class="wp-block-shiro-stair stair">
        <h2 class="stair__heading is-style-h3">
         Partner for change
        </h2>
        <img class="wp-image-61194 size-image_16x9_small stair__image" height="338" loading="lazy" src="https://wikimediafoundation.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/KatherineMaher_IvanMartinez_MexicanCultureMinistry_partnership-e1565971800729.jpg?w=600&amp;h=338&amp;crop=1" width="600"/>
        <p class="stair__body">
         Working with partners like Google, the
         <a href="/2018/04/19/wikimedia-the-met-shared-digital-vision/">
          Metropolitan Museum of Art
         </a>
         , and UNESCO, Wikimedia actively seeks new knowledge, closing systemic gaps in global understanding of language, culture, and identity.
        </p>
       </div>
       <div class="wp-block-shiro-stair stair">
        <h2 class="stair__heading is-style-h3">
         Open the Knowledge
        </h2>
        <img alt="Mirror views of groups of people" class="wp-image-65288 size-image_16x9_small stair__image" height="683" loading="lazy" sizes="(max-width: 1024px) 100vw, 1024px" src="https://wikimediafoundation.org/wp-content/uploads/2021/09/DEI_Representation.png" srcset="https://wikimediafoundation.org/wp-content/uploads/2021/09/DEI_Representation.png 1024w, https://wikimediafoundation.org/wp-content/uploads/2021/09/DEI_Representation.png?resize=300,200 300w, https://wikimediafoundation.org/wp-content/uploads/2021/09/DEI_Representation.png?resize=768,512 768w" width="1024"/>
        <p class="stair__body">
         <a href="https://wikimediafoundation.org/about/open-the-knowledge/">
          “Open the Knowledge”
         </a>
         is our call to everyone to promote radical knowledge equity, creating a living record of history, stories, and contexts for and by all people. It aims to make the Wikimedia movement more diverse, equitable, and inclusive.
        </p>
