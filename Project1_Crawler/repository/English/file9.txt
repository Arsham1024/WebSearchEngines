         <span class="mw-headline" data-mw-comment='{"type":"heading","level":0,"id":"h-Meta-Wiki","replies":["h-November_2021-Meta-Wiki","h-October_2021-Meta-Wiki","h-September_2021-Meta-Wiki"],"headingLevel":1,"placeholderHeading":false}' id="Meta-Wiki">

        </h1>
        <div class="MainPage__block_contents" id="mf-intro">
         <p>
          <b>
           Welcome to
           <a href="/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Meta:About" title="Special:MyLanguage/Meta:About">
            Meta-Wiki
           </a>
          </b>
          , the global community site for the
          <a href="/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Wikimedia_Foundation" title="Special:MyLanguage/Wikimedia Foundation">
           Wikimedia Foundation's
          </a>
          <a href="/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Wikimedia_projects" title="Special:MyLanguage/Wikimedia projects">
           projects
          </a>
          and related projects, from coordination and documentation to planning and analysis.
         </p>
         <p>
          Other meta-focused wikis such as
          <a class="extiw" href="https://outreach.wikimedia.org/wiki/" title="outreach:">
           Wikimedia Outreach
          </a>
          are specialized projects that have their roots in Meta-Wiki.  Related discussions also take place on Wikimedia
          <a href="/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Mailing_lists/Overview" title="Special:MyLanguage/Mailing lists/Overview">
           mailing lists
          </a>
          (particularly
          <b>
           <a href="/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Mailing_lists#Wikimedia_mailing_list" title="Special:MyLanguage/Mailing lists">
            wikimedia-l
           </a>
          </b>
          , with its low-traffic equivalent
          <a href="/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Mailing_lists#Wikimedia_Announcements_mailing_list" title="Special:MyLanguage/Mailing lists">
           WikimediaAnnounce
          </a>
          ),
          <a href="/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/IRC" title="Special:MyLanguage/IRC">
           IRC channels
          </a>
          on Libera, individual wikis of
          <a href="/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Wikimedia_movement_affiliates" title="Special:MyLanguage/Wikimedia movement affiliates">
           Wikimedia affiliates
          </a>
          , and other places.
         </p>

           </a>

           <a class="extiw" href="https://www.mediawiki.org/wiki/" title="mw:">
            MediaWiki
           </a>
           . Meta-Wiki is the coordination wiki for the various Wikimedia wikis.
